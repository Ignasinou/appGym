About Palestrapp

Palestrapp is a straightforward workout tracking app designed to make logging exercises and visualizing workout history as easy as possible. Its minimalist design and intuitive interface ensure you can focus on your fitness journey without unnecessary distractions.

Main Features

Here’s what makes Palestrapp stand out:

  • Home Screen: Navigate effortlessly between "Workout" and "History" with a clean, monochrome layout.
  • Workout Recording: Log exercises with smart suggestions based on your past workouts.
  • History Visualization: A calendar view highlighting workout days and providing quick access to details.
  • Data Persistence: Your data is automatically saved, ensuring nothing gets lost.
